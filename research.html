Research interests

Economic modelling of telecommunications, mobile and wireless communications networks, control and automation systems, internet of things, artificial intelligence and machine learning, signal processing, game theory, and business models.

Research projects

  1. National University of San Antonio Abad of Cusco, Peru
    12/2025 - present: Design and simulation of a PI controller applied to a permanent magnet synchronous motor This project aims to design and simulate a PI controller for a permanent magnet synchronous motor, improving stability and performance in speed or position control. The system will be modelled and evaluated through simulation in order to validate an efficient, low-cost control strategy for industrial and electric mobility applications.

  2. Universitat Politècnica de València, Spain
    09/2022 - 07/2024: Radio access network evolution towards 6G for massive and low latency services This project has focused on addressing the limitations of current 5G networks to support future Internet of Everything (IoE) applications. The aim is to define and develop new generations of wireless networks (B5G and 6G) capable of seamlessly integrating IoE functionalities.

    09/2019 - 09/2022: Dynamic Network Slicing in 5G Radio Access (ATLAS) This project has addressed the management of physical and virtual resource allocation by the infrastructure provider to tenants, as well as the business models associated with this provider-tenant relationship. This project has been funded by the European Union (EU) and the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation.
