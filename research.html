Research interests

Economic modelling of telecommunications, mobile and wireless communications networks, control and automation systems, internet of things, artificial intelligence and machine learning, signal processing, game theory, and business models.

Research projects

  1. Universitat Politècnica de València, España
    09/2022 - presente: Radio access network evolution towards 6G for massive and low latency services This project has focused on addressing the limitations of current 5G networks to support future Internet of Everything (IoE) applications. The aim is to define and develop new generations of wireless networks (B5G and 6G) capable of seamlessly integrating IoE functionalities.
    09/2019 - 09/2022: Dynamic Network Slicing in 5G Radio Access (ATLAS) This project has addressed the management of physical and virtual resource allocation by the infrastructure provider to tenants, as well as the business models associated with this provider-tenant relationship. This project has been funded by the European Union (EU) and the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation.
  2. Dynamic Network Slicing in 5G Radio Access (ATLAS)
    09/2019 - 09/2022
    Universitat Politècnica de València, España

    Este proyecto ha abordado la gestión de la asignación de recursos físicos y virtuales por parte del proveedor de infraestructuras a los inquilinos, así como los modelos de negocio asociados a esta relación proveedor-inquilino. Este proyecto ha sido financiado por la Unión Europea (UE) y el Ministerio de Ciencia e Innovación español.
