I am currently a Professor and Researcher in Telecommunications, Networks and Business Models. My research focuses on telecommunications economics, network theory and innovative business models, with applications in 5G and 6G networks and the Internet of Things (IoT). In addition, I explore emerging technologies such as Network Slicing and game theory applied to mobile networks.

I hold a PhD in Telecommunications, a Master's Degree in Strategic Telecommunications Management, and an MBA in Business Management. I have worked on research projects in Spain at the Polytechnic University of Valencia, in France at IMT Atlantique, and in Peru. I have also taught at the Salesian Polytechnic University.

If you are interested in collaborating on research on 5G, IoT, or telecommunications, please do not hesitate to contact me.
