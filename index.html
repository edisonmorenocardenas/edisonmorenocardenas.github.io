I am currently a Reserarch professor at the Professional School of Electronic Engineering at the National University of San Antonio Abad in Cusco, Peru, and a RENACYT Level III Researcher recognized by the National Council of Science, Technology and Technological Innovation (CONCYTEC). My research focuses on wireless communications, 5G and 6G mobile networks, network economics, the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence, autonomous systems, unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), computer vision, intelligent sensing, and control systems. My work aims to develop interdisciplinary solutions that integrate communication technologies with intelligent systems to address emerging challenges in next-generation engineering.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Telecommunications from the Department of Communications at Universitat Politècnica de València , Spain. Also, I hold an Electronic Engineering degree from the National University of San Antonio Abad in Cusco, an M.Sc. degree in Strategic Management of Telecommunications from the National University of San Marcos , and a Master's Degree in Business Management (MBA) degree from the University of Valencia. I have worked on research projects at Universitat Politècnica de València, Spain, IMT Atlantique, France, and Peru. Also, I have taught at Universidad Politécnica Salesiana (UPS).

Academic Editor at Wiley for the International Journal of Antennas and Propagation and Reviewing Editor within the Springer Nature Reviewer Program, actively contributing to peer review, editorial assessment, and the maintenance of scientific quality standards. Author and co-author of scientific publications indexed in WoS and Scopus in high-impact international journals. Scientific reviewer for editorials such as IEEE, Elsevier, Wiley, and Springer Nature. Member of the IEEE Communications Society (IEEE ComSoc).

In addition to my research activities, I teach undergraduate and postgraduate courses in electronic engineering and telecommunications, supervise research projects, and mentor students in engineering and applied research. I am committed to fostering interdisciplinary collaboration between academia and industry, whilst promoting innovation through research, education, and technology transfer.

I welcome collaboration with researchers, universities, research centers, and industry partners interested in wireless communications, smart systems, artificial intelligence, the IoT, autonomous systems, UAVs, and next-generation networks. I encourage prospective postgraduate students and research collaborators to get in touch with me.
